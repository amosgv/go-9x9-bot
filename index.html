Bot reasoning

Your move

Object

Control more territory than your opponent. Capture stones along the way.

Placement

Stones go on intersections, not in squares. Black plays first. Once placed, stones don't move.

Liberties

Empty points orthogonally adjacent to a stone or connected group. A group with zero liberties is captured and removed.

No suicide

You can't play a move that leaves your own group with zero liberties — unless that move first captures opponent stones.

Ko (劫)

You may not make a move that recreates a previous board position. This prevents infinite recapture loops.

Ending and scoring

Two consecutive passes end the game. Score = territory + captures. White typically receives 5.5–7 komi on a 9×9 board.
